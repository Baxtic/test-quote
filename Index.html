As the heart of Uzbekistan and one of Central Asia’s most vibrant cities, Tashkent blends rich cultural heritage with modern energy, offering endless opportunities to discover, shop, and experience something new every day.

Tashkent Travel Guide
Tashkent is located in eastern Uzbekistan, nestled in the Tashkent region near the border with Kazakhstan. The city is served by Islam Karimov Tashkent International Airport, the country's main international gateway. Tashkent is known for its wide blend of Soviet architecture, modern urban design, and traditional Uzbek charm.
Downtown Tashkent is the city's business and cultural hub, where green trees meet leafy avenues and cozy tea houses. Old Tashkent, including districts like Kukcha and Eski Shahar, offers a glimpse into the city's historic past, with mosques, madrassahs, and authentic local shops.
